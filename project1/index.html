We are a modest, come-as-you-are joint that has been serving hearty American breakfasts, lunches and weekend brunches in downtown New Paltz since 1993! Please join us on your visit to New Paltz and call us for more info or if you have any questions!

Don’t have the time to visit us or don’t feel like driving? Check out our Doordash page and get your brunch delivered to you!
